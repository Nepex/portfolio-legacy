Cascade
Cascade was a browser game I made back in 2016. It was inspired by JRPGs (primarily older Final Fantasy games). I designed tile-based maps with Tiled which allowed the user to freely walk around with an animated sprite. Cascade featured a fully-fledged random encounter and battle system. The user could recruit a variety of classes to their party that specialized in either physical damage, magic damage, or healing. Cascade also featured shops, quests, inventory, equipment management, scenes, a messaging system, and a world map overview. It was made responsive to be compatible with mobile and desktop.
Made using...
Angular, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, PHP, mySQLi
